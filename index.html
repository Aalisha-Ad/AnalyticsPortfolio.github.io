Nexus Retail Group (SQL)

The client was concerned about rising inventory holding costs, uncertain promotional effectiveness, and the impact of economic factors such as inflation and GDP fluctuations on product demand and profitability. Using SQL-based exploratory and business analysis, we examined sales transactions, inventory levels, product categories, promotional campaigns, seasonal trends, and macroeconomic indicators. The analysis revealed that Electronics carried the highest inventory burden, tying up over $2.2 million in working capital, while promotional campaigns showed limited impact on increasing sales volume.

PureSip Beverages Sales Analysis (PowerBI)

PureSip Beverages, a distributor of brands such as Coke, Fanta, and Sprite, needed a more efficient way to manage sales data from multiple regional locations. The company relied on separate spreadsheets, making data consolidation slow, error-prone, and difficult to analyze. To solve this, a Power BI solution was implemented by connecting data from local folders, centralizing information, and automating data refreshes. This reduced manual workload, improved data accuracy, and provided valuable sales insights. It was recommended that the company standardize data collection processes and continue leveraging Power BI for advanced reporting and decision-making.

OmniStep Footwear using PowerBI

Led the design and implementation of an enterprise Power BI solution for a multinational footwear retailer operating across six global markets and generating $47M in annual revenue. Built a centralized analytics platform by integrating data from multiple ERP systems, regional databases, and e-commerce platforms. Developed a scalable star-schema model, 47 advanced DAX measures, and executive dashboards covering revenue performance, profitability analysis, customer segmentation, and inventory insights. The solution replaced fragmented spreadsheet reporting, reduced reporting time by 90%, identified $2.1M in margin leakage, uncovered $1.2M growth opportunities, and enabled data-driven decision-making supporting an expected 8–12% margin improvement.

Salient Venture Sales Analysis using Excel

The client experienced a 20% revenue decline every quarter driven by poor inventory management and low customer retention. We suggested some strategies to apply before the forth quarter. Conducted an end-to-end sales analysis to evaluate the effectiveness of strategic initiatives implemented after three consecutive quarters of revenue decline. Using Excel, analyzed Q4 transaction-level data to assess the impact of performance-based incentives, inventory diversification and regional marketing expansion on business performance. The analysis revealed a revenue recovery to $112.7M, a 15% profit margin and strong contributions from new product categories and emerging regional markets.
